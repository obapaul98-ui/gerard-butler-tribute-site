Why were there two Greenland movies?

The confusion arose because Gerard Butler has **two projects** bearing the Greenland name:
1. Greenland (2020): The highly successful original sci-fi disaster thriller where he plays John Garrity as a family struggles to survive a cataclysmic comet.
2. Greenland: Migration (Sequel): The upcoming highly anticipated theatrical sequel, scheduled to begin production. You previously had both entries in your list, which caused duplicate entries. We have safely streamlined the site to display only the original, completed **Greenland (2020)**.

What are the poster differences?

- poster_greenland.jpg: The active poster image loaded by films.html.
- poster_greenland_migration.jpg: The promotional art/poster for the upcoming sequel, which was accidentally set as the default on some pages.
- User Screenshots: The four screenshots uploaded during your feedback representing the box office and original promotional posters that you want active.
